This section of the assistant aims at building a catalog of Attack Modes.

Manage Systems
Where the analyst can create a system and describe the Threat Model associated with it.
Manage System's Attacks
Tool that help compiling a list of attacks that appears specifically on this system. The list of available Attacks is contrained by the Threat Model
View SCREAM Analysis Results
The results of the SCREAM analysis of a system are compiled here. It displays the STC allowed by the Threat Model along with the results of the attacks that are linked to the said system.
